Design Principles

PARC: Contrast

PetCo

petco.com

PetCo does a great job of utilizing contrast on their website. The white font against the darker background helps to make not only their navigation bar stand out, but also helps their sale selection stand out to the user so they can navigate quickly and efficiently to either one.

White Space

Zoom

zoom.com

Zoom does a great job of utilizing white space in their website. Since most of their background is made up of white space, this creates a user-friendly experience and helps those who use their application, such as busy professionals to quickly navigate to either join or host a virtual meeting.

PARC: Repitition

Old Navy

oldnavy.com

Old Navy does a great job of utilizing many of the principles on their website, but I believe the most noticeable one they utilized is repitition. They utilize the same color palette of red, white, and bluw and as you scroll down the format of the catagories continues.
