Target Audience

The target audience for LCW are individuals ages 24-40 who live or work in Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven, Idaho. They utilize the LCW site to help them prepare for their daily commutes and outings.

Personas

Mckenzie Floyd

Mckenzie is a young professional who currently lives in Preston, but commutes Monday-Friday to Soda Springs. Since she drives for almost 2 hours each day it is important for her to check weather conditions especially in the winter so she knows if she might need to allot more time due to snow.

Phillip Rivers

Philip is from Fish Haven, Idaho. As a stay-at-home dad to three daughters, he is responsible for bringing his oldest daughter, Zoey to Kindergarten. It is important he check weather daily so he can make sure the girls dress accordingly for their walks to and from school.

Scenerios

  • Mckenzie needs to be able to see if the weather in Soda Springs, Idaho where she works is different than in Preston, Idaho where she lives. Will she be able to do this?

    • Yes, of course! Mckenzie will be able to see the weather of both locations.

  • Phillip is often busy in the mornings getting his daughters ready for the day. Will be be able to view weather conditions the night before so he can prepare in advance?

    • Yes, Lowcountry Weather will have a daily and weekly forecast so Phillip can prepare for upcoming outings in advance.

  • Mckenzie often only utilizes her phone for viewing websites. Will she be able to see all the information she needs on this device?

    • Yes. Mckenzie will be able to view Lowcountry weather efficiently from her cell phone, a tablet, or a computer.

  • Phillip plans to take his family to Pocatello, Idaho for the weekend. Will he be able to view the weather there?

    • No, unfortunately Lowcountry Weather does not have weather data available for Pocatello, Idaho; however we do plan to expand our service to other locations in the future.

  • Mckenzie has plans to go snowmobiling near Fish Haven, Idaho this weekend. Will she be able to view the weather conditions there?

    • Yes, Lowcountry Weather offers weather information for Fish Haven, Idaho. Mckenzie will not only be able to view the upcoming weather conditions, but will also be able to see if they have enough snow to successfully go snowmobiling before she arrives!
