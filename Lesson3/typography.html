Typography

The two fonts that I chose were: Trebuchet MS and Sansita.


Trebuchet MS

Trebuchet MS is a sans-serif typeface that I chose to utilize for my headers. I think it is a strong typeface that isn't overpowering when utlized in larger text sizes.

Sample text of Trebuchet MS.


Sansita

Sansita is a font that borders between typography, calligraphy and lettering. I really enjoy this font and feel like it paired well with Trebuchet MS as my main font.
