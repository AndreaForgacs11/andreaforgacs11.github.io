One of the Warmest Winters on Record

This winter will likely be among the top 10 warmest winters since 1945, but is abnormal weather becoming the new normal? While it is still February, the meteorological winter is nearing its end. Regardless of how we define winter, the last few months have been abnormally warm across the valley. In fact, it has been so unusually warm that the 2020-2021 winter has made its way into the record books on more than one occasion lately.
