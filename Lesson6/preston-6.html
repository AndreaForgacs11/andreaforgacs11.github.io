One of the Warmest Winters on Record

This winter in Preson, ID will likely be among the top 10 warmest winters since 1945, but is abnormal weather becoming the new normal?

While it is still only February, the meteorological winter is nearing its end (December 21-March 20). Regardless of how we define winter, the last few months have certainly been abnormally warm across Preston, ID valley.

In fact, it has been so unusually warm that the 2020-2021 winter has made its way into the record books on more than one occasion lately.

Among these records are 8 days this season that have brought the warmest temperatures since record keeping first began. These include:

  • January 1
  • January 5
  • January 6
  • January 11
  • January 22
  • January 23
  • February 1
  • February 5

As the end of the season continues and warm temperatures continue to be the new norm we will soon see how this might affect the spring and summer season with a lack of much needed moisture.
